Stop Skinning Stars

Option 1

PixiJS

Best practical match for a living 2D reef. It gives us sprites, procedural shapes, filters, particles, hit testing, and WebGL speed while keeping the UI in normal HTML.

StrengthOrganic scene composition, glow, particles, custom sprites, thousands of marks.
WeaknessWe own layout, accessibility overlays, and asset pipeline.
Best fit for Coral reef

Option 3

Konva

A friendly 2D canvas scene graph. It is easier than PixiJS for dragging and events, but less capable for reef atmosphere, particle shimmer, and shader-like depth.

Decision

Pick the Medium First

The reef metaphor needs a scene renderer, not a node-link graph renderer. PixiJS is the best next prototype because it can stay 2D, expressive, performant, and shippable.
